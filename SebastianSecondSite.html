Basic Skateboarding Terminology

Hello, this website will teach you the basics of skating terminology



Frontside Backside Regular Goofy Switch Fakie Nollie
Frontside is where you or the board rotates
so your are facing the direction of motion. 		Backside is where you or the board rotates
so that your back faces the direction of motion. 		Regular is the stance where your left foot
is in front on the board. 		Goofy is when you ride with your right foot first.
About half of skaters ride this way. 		Switch is when you ride in the
opposite stance (the one that feels weird). 		Fake is when you ride so that
you're in your normal stance but you're
going backwards. (Your foot's on the tail) 		Nollie is when you ride so that
you're in normal stance but with your front
foot on the nose.
